I am a Post-Doctoral researcher in the Center for Speech Technology Research (CSTR), which is part of the Institute for Language Cognition and Computation (ILCC) in the School of Informatics at The University of Edinburgh.

My research interests are Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning with Speech and Language Processing applications. I am currently working on the Ultrax Speech project. My tasks involve exploring novel Machine Learning methods to exploit ultrasound and audio signals from children with Speech Sound Disorders. I am also interested in prosody modeling for Text-to-Speech synthesis. During my PhD, I explored novel ways to improve the naturalness of Statistical Parametric Speech Synthesis using deep neural network architectures that could leverage the hierarchical structure of prosody.

Key interests: Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Automatic Speech Recognition, Speech Synthesis, Prosody.
